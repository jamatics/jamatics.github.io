About Me

I am a passionate robotic technologist who is working as Senior Researcher at AIMEN Technology center in Galicia, Spian. Before joining AIMEN, I was working as team lead at Automotive Technology Centre of Galicia - Spain (CTAG). Before joining CTAG, I was holding postdoctoral research positions at Italian Institute of Technology - Italy, Rice University - USA and Halmstad University - Sweden. I participated in the prominent research projects such as COVR, EUROBENCH, ROBOMATE, NG-TEST, and PICAV across Europe and US. I have been a design engineer and quality assurance engineer at AE-Design and KSB Pumps. I holds PhD and master in robotics from University of Genoa-Italy. BS in mechanical engineering from University of Engineering and Technology Lahore - Pakistan.
